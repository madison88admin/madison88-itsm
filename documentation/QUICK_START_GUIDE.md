# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Madison88 ITSM Platform! This section will help you get started in just a few minutes.

## 1. Logging In
- Go to https://itsm.madison88.com
- Enter your company email and password
- Click Login

## 2. Creating a Ticket
- Click "New Ticket" in the menu
- Fill in the required details (category, title, description)
- Click Submit

## 3. Tracking Your Ticket
- Click "Tickets" to see your requests
- Click any ticket to view details and status

## 4. Using the Knowledge Base
- Click "Knowledge Base" in the menu
- Search for solutions to common problems

For more details, see the full user manual below.
